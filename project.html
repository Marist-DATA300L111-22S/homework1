When we talk about sports, the term G.O.A.T tends to come up when talking about great players. G.O.A.T means greatest of all time. In the game of basketball when we talk about the greatest of all time three names are consistenly brought up. Michael Jordan, Lebron James and Kobe Bryant are considered three of the greatest basketball players ever. We are going to take a look at the best 5 years of these players careers. By comparing 5 of their best years playing basketball, I think we can get a better understanding, of who was the most dominant during their prime.
